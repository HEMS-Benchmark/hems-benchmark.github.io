About Us

Driving the Future of Energy AI

We are committed to providing professional evaluation tools and standards for artificial intelligence development in the energy management field. Through our open-source benchmark framework, we aim to promote collaboration between academia and industry, accelerating innovation and application of intelligent energy technologies.

Our project is inspired by excellent power industry benchmarks like ElecBench, while incorporating the latest trends in large language models and agentic AI technologies, providing more professional and comprehensive evaluation solutions for the energy management domain.

Join us in building a smarter, more efficient, and more sustainable energy future!
